We may collect the following information:

  • Name

  • Phone number

2. How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Provide conversational messaging services

3. SMS and Messaging Privacy

If you provide your mobile number or message Lyra, you consent to receive 1:1 conversational text messages related to your interaction with the service, including replies, reminders, and scheduling assistance.

Message frequency varies.
Message and data rates may apply.
Reply STOP to opt out.
Reply HELP for help.

We do not sell, rent, or share mobile opt-in data, SMS consent, or phone numbers with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes.

4. How Consent Works

Users opt in by initiating contact themselves or by directly requesting SMS follow-up, reminders, or conversational assistance from Lyra. We do not send unsolicited marketing text messages.

We may share information only as needed to:

These service providers are only given access as necessary to perform their functions and are expected to safeguard your information.

6. Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as reasonably necessary to provide the service, maintain records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations.

8. Your Choices

You may:
