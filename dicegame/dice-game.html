How to Play

  • Dice progression: clear each die in order: d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, then d20.
  • Easy mode: safer, slower skill point gain. Failed rolls do not reset die progression, and you gain 1 skill point per 250 score earned.
  • Normal mode: riskier, faster skill point gain. Failed rolls reset die progression to the beginning, and you gain 1 skill point per 100 score earned.
  • Skill points: awarded continuously during gameplay as your lifetime score crosses each mode threshold.
  • Failed rolls: if your roll is below the required value, you fail that step (and reset to start in normal mode).
  • Dev mode: enter devmode as the player name to open developer tools.
