How SMS Consent Works

  • The user enters their phone number in the website form.
  • The user manually checks the consent checkbox.
  • The user submits the form.
  • Only users who opt in are sent SMS messages.
  • Users can reply STOP to unsubscribe and HELP for help.

Compliance Notes

  • Opt-in is collected through a public website form.
  • Consent checkbox is unchecked by default.
  • Privacy Policy and Terms & Conditions are linked on the page.
  • Message frequency varies.
  • Message and data rates may apply.
  • Users can reply STOP to opt out and HELP for help.
  • Mobile opt-in data is not shared with third parties.
